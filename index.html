About Kian E. Jamasbi

Kian E. Jamasbi (born 1999) is an American law student, author, and entrepreneur. He is currently pursuing a Juris Doctor at Fordham School of Law with a concentration in International, Comparative, and Foreign Law. Jamasbi is the co-founder and Managing Member of WorldLaw LLC.

Education

Jamasbi earned a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where he worked as a Research Assistant at the Institute for Social Research, focusing on socioeconomic studies. He also worked as a Legal Assistant at Conklin Benham, PC. Before his undergraduate studies, Jamasbi graduated cum laude from Germantown Academy, receiving awards for poetry and mathematics and representing the school at the Pennsylvania Scholastic Chess Championships.

Jamasbi is an active member of the Pennsylvania Bar Association, contributing to committees on Charitable Organizations, Alternative Dispute Resolution, and Technology. He is also affiliated with the Iranian American Bar Association and the New York State Bar Association.
