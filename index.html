Final Words on 2024 Election

October 23, 2024 - New York City, New York

Hello, my name is Kian. I am a law student, advocate, and entrepreneur. With less than two weeks remaining in this election cycle, I wanted to share my final ballot decisions.

Presidential Election

Building on my earlier endorsement of Robert F. Kennedy Jr. for President, I reaffirm my belief in the need to challenge the entrenched two-party system. My write-in vote for Robert F. Kennedy Jr. reflects my continued commitment to that challenge. While Kennedy has recently urged his supporters to vote for former President Donald Trump, my support remains, having contributed $225 to Team Kennedy throughout this cycle, even as Kennedy’s stance evolved. Some say voting for Kennedy, even if successful, only adds another elector for Trump. This may be true, but it reflects the choices we have as free voters.

Pennsylvania Races

For U.S. Senator, I voted for Robert P. Casey Jr. (Democrat), valuing his Pennsylvania roots and consistent support for nuclear arms control. This choice also aims to prevent a potential GOP trifecta at the federal level, given the anticipated red wave this November.

In the Attorney General race, I supported Eric L. Settle (Forward Party), aiming to help the Forward Party achieve major-party status by reaching the 2% threshold. For fiscal oversight positions, I chose Republicans Tim DeFoor and Stacy Garrity for Auditor General and State Treasurer, respectively.

In both the 4th Congressional District and 70th District General Assembly races, I wrote in my own name.
