Pennsylvania Unemployment Compensation Board of Review

Jamasbi v. Gold Medal Karate Inc., No. 2024018904-RO (Pa. UCBR 2024), affirmed an important precedent in nonprofit wage law by protecting workers against erratic pay.

Federal Trade Commission

Jamasbi v. Adecco Recruitment, FTC Report No. 177174559 (Sept. 1, 2024), involved a suspected fraudulent job offer via unsolicited text.

Jamasbi v. Henry Jones, FTC Report No. 177789856 (Sept. 17, 2024), involving a sextortion scam email that demanded $1,300 in cryptocurrency.

Pennsylvania Attorney General, Bureau of Consumer Protection

Jamasbi v. Gold Medal Karate Inc., No. BCP-24-05-003960 (Pa. Bureau of Consumer Protection Feb. 9, 2024), involved a complaint concerning the unauthorized use of the plaintiff’s work email and identity by Gold Medal Karate Inc. after his departure from the company. Mediation efforts focused on ensuring the proper closure of the plaintiff’s work email and public misrepresentation of his personal information.

Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry

Jamasbi v. Gold Medal Karate, No. 2024-00130 (Pa. Dep't of Labor & Indus., Bureau of Labor Law Compliance Jan. 30, 2024), involved a wage claim against Gold Medal Karate for failure to pay legally entitled wages, in violation of Pennsylvania’s Wage Payment and Collection Law, 43 P.S. §§ 260.1 – 260.12. The Bureau of Labor Law Compliance was unable to resolve the claim.
