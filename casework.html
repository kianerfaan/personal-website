Casework

  • In re Henry Jones, FTC Report No. 177789856 (Sept. 17, 2024), addressing a sextortion scam email that demanded $1,300 in cryptocurrency, representing a violation of consumer protection laws.
  • In re Adecco Recruitment, FTC Report No. 177174559 (Sept. 1, 2024), involving a suspected fraudulent job offer delivered via unsolicited text message, raising concerns under the FTC’s deceptive practices jurisdiction.
  • Jamasbi v. Gold Medal Karate Inc. dba Zhang-Sah Martial Arts, No. 2024018904-RO (Pa. UCBR 2024), affirming protections for nonprofit workers regarding erratic wage payments, marking a significant precedent in nonprofit wage law.
  • In re Gold Medal Karate Inc. dba Zhang-Sah Martial Arts, No. BCP-24-05-003960 (Pa. Bureau of Consumer Protection Feb. 9, 2024), involving the unauthorized use of a former employee's work email and identity following their departure from the company, in violation of consumer protection statutes.
  • In re Gold Medal Karate Inc. dba Zhang-Sah Martial Arts, No. 2024-00130 (Pa. Dep't of Labor & Indus., Bureau of Labor Law Compliance Jan. 30, 2024), addressing a wage claim against Gold Medal Karate for failure to pay legally entitled wages, implicating violations of Pennsylvania’s Wage Payment and Collection Law.
