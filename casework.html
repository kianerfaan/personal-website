Casework

Pennsylvania Unemployment

Jamasbi v. Gold Medal Karate Inc., No. 2024018904-RO (Pa. UCBR 2024), affirmed an important precedent in nonprofit wage law by protecting workers against erratic pay.

Federal Trade Commission

Jamasbi v. Adecco Recruitment, FTC Report No. 177174559 (Sept. 1, 2024), involved a suspected fraudulent job offer via unsolicited text, reinforcing concerns about employment scams promising high pay for minimal requirements.
